Story

I started out as a web developer and UX Designer more than 15 years ago.
Soon I realised that to really design great customer experiences one needs to make decisions at the business model level. So I joined mBank.pl as a product manager and grew with a very innovative challenger brand in what now is called the "fintech" space.

I was propelled into a general management career culminating in a leadership role in an international expansion, managing portfolio of products and a marketing department. At the same time I wrote a PhD on the topic of trust in the digital economy and completed an MBA at INSEAD.

After 7+ years in leadership positions in a fast growth environment I moved to Australia. I started working in innovation and strategy space and discovered great potential in combining design thinking and classic strategy frameworks. In the last few years I've worked as a consultant with some great leaders on transforming their companies in face of disruption.
